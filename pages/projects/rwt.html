Radioactive Waste Training

  • Overview: An action roguelike game.
  • Made with: Godot, GDScript
  • Team project (4 people)
  • Role: Programmer, Lead Developer, Level Designer
  • Development: Complete
  • Origin: Final project for my game development class.
  • Technical challenges:
    • Procedural level generation: utilized a variation on the flood-fill algorithm that randomly chooses the next room location around the map's edge.
    • Team accountability: Because I had the most game development experience in the team, I took leadership role and made sure everyone was being held accountable and helping whenever it was needed.
